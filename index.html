Our story

About VÉRA

VÉRA was born in Milan with a simple belief: skincare should feel as honest as it is effective. We craft clean, cruelty-free, unisex formulas inspired by Italian botanical rituals and modern science.

Each product is designed to work across skin types, especially the sensitive and reactive. We focus on pared-back routines that let true skin stay visible and vibrant.

True beauty, true care, true simplicity guide every decision we make.

  • Clean ingredients sourced responsibly
  • Cruelty-free & vegan friendly
  • Gender-inclusive formulations
  • Designed for sensitive skin
